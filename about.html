About Glory International School

Glory International, located in Dansoman, Ghana, is a co-educational institution known for its strong academic performance and vibrant extracurricular programs. Established in 1968, it provides a nurturing environment for students, emphasizing discipline, innovation, and community involvement. The school offers diverse programs, including General Arts, Sciences, and Business, preparing students for higher education and career success. Its motto, “Truth, Excellence, and Service,” reflects its commitment to holistic education.
